What is a 'Shared Resource'?

Shared resources refers to multiple people being able to use the resources you have been allocated. With PRO, no one else is able to use the resources you're allocated - which means your resources would be 'dedicated'.

What is meant by 'Daily Restarts'?

Daily restarts occur with our Forever-Free plan. If you do not have a PRO subscription, your server will automatically shut down every 24 hours to free up space for users on our free clusters. With PRO, servers do not automatically shut down daily.

What is 'Bring Your Own Software'?

BYOS (Bring Your Own Software) is a feature which allows PRO users to bring their own server type to our platform. While our free tier only offers Minecraft Paper, going PRO means you could run servers such as CS:GO, Minecraft Bungeecord, Rust and more.

What does 'Keep Your Free Server' mean?

Keep Your Free Server means that you can choose to create a whole new server for your PRO subscription, and leave your existing server as-is for other uses such as testing or running another service.

Are Custom Plans available?

Absolutely. We can offer PRO plans with up to 128gb of DDR4 memory, 32 threads and 1TB of NVMe storage. Big networks who choose a custom plan can benefit from huge savings against competitors or even by managing their own dedicated server. Feel free to get in touch at sales@cxmpute.com to discuss a custom plan.

Can I refer people to PRO for a discount?

Yes! Referring someone to PRO gives you 50% (yes, really) of their first month. If you refer someone to a custom plan which costs $20, you'd recieve $10. No fees, no wait times. It's literally free money.
